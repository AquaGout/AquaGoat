Cabriela

Where thrilling challenges and captivating discoveries await!

Are you ready to dive into an exciting and magical adventure in the depths of the sea?

Join our hybrid heroine, the goat-mermaid, and discover an underwater world full of dangers and wonders!

Beware!

Keep safe from the fierce sharks lurking in the depths! You must dodge their sharp fins and teeth as you glide towards your destination.
Majestic manta rays will also cross your path. Stay calm and avoid their graceful movements to keep progressing towards the ocean floor.
Beware of the floating jellyfish with their long tentacles. Steer clear of their stinging tendrils to ensure a safe journey through the underwater realm.

How to play

Navigate the Goat-Mermaid using the left and right arrow keys to evade enemies. Colliding with an enemy results in game over. Test your survival skills and aim to endure for as long as possible. Best of luck in this challenging adventure!
